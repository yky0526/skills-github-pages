Fun facts about cats

1. Vocal Variety: Cats can make over 100 different sounds, while dogs typically make about 10. They use these sounds to communicate different emotions and needs.

2. Sleeping Champions: Cats sleep for an average of 13 to 16 hours a day, making them one of the sleepiest mammals. Some cats can sleep up to 20 hours in a 24-hour period!

3. Superb Jumpers: Cats are amazing jumpers! They can leap up to six times their body length in a single bound due to their powerful leg muscles.

4. Night Vision: Cats can see in light levels six times lower than what a human needs. Their eyes have a special membrane called the tapetum lucidum, which reflects light and helps them see in the dark.

5. Scent Marking: Cats have scent glands on their cheeks and paws. They often rub their faces against objects (including humans) to mark their territory with their scent.
